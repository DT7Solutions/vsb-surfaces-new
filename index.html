About The VSB Group

At VSB, we take pride in being a 100% Export Oriented Unit (EOU) specializing in the production and global distribution of high-quality quartz and granite surfaces. With a strong presence across the USA, Europe, and the Middle East, we cater to a diverse range of residential and commercial projects—from sleek kitchen countertops and elegant bathroom vanities to durable backsplashes, living space surfaces, and architectural wall claddings.

Driven by a commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainability, our operations blend advanced manufacturing technology like Breton (Italy) with the timeless beauty of natural and engineered stone. Every slab we produce meets international quality standards, ensuring long-lasting aesthetics and functionality for spaces across the globe.

Our collections are curated to meet the demands of luxury living—where aesthetics meet performance, and every detail reflects sophistication. From contemporary minimalism to classic opulence, VSB is the trusted partner of designers, architects, and developers who seek nothing less than excellence.

Elevate your spaces with the enduring luxury of stone. Crafted to inspire. Designed to last.
