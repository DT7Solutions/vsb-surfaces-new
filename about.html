About the Company

India’s Trusted Natural & Engineered Stone Exporter

VSB Group is an exporter and distributor of the finest natural and engineered stones in India. We work with some of the top talents in the country to provide you with the best selection of the highest quality natural stone available. VSB Group is committed to offering a broad range of unique and exclusive Natural Stones, facilitating the ideal choice of materials in today’s world of architecture and interior design. Over the years, we have undergone progressive growth and strong expansion, becoming a leader in the field and contributing to the industry with innovation and technology.
